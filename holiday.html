F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where do I apply for a grant?

    To enroll in a course, head over to (https://learn.mathisify.org) and register yourself as a student. Once completed, select a course, make sure that you are registered and signed in.

  • Where do I find the course material for each chapter?

    You can find the course material for each chapter on our learn page (https://learn.mathisify.org). Under each chapter (course), there will be informational articles explaining each concept/topic in that unit.

  • Can I enroll in more than one course at a time?

    Yes you can! You can enroll in as many courses as you desire, but we suggest limiting yourself to 1-2 at a time, so you can attend weekly workshops and keep up with the workload.

  • How much does each course/workshop cost?

    Each course/workshop is completely free! All activities included. At Mathisi-fy, we strive to provide a solid computer science education for anyone, anywhere in the world.
